About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Case Western Reserve University, where I work on Generative AI and High Performance Computing (HPC), Deep Learning algorithms with applications in performace anomaly detection and diagnostic in large scalse HPC systems, Large Language Models(LLMs) privacy enhauncement, and differential privacy in data sharing. My research focuses on developing robust deep learning models that can work with limited labeled data and transfer effectively across various large scale domains.

Prior to my PhD, I worked as a research and development engineer at Tech Company, where I developed efficient software solutions for real-world problems. I received my BSc in Computer Engineering from Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at University of Tehran.

My research interests include:

  • Generative AI
  • LLM Privacy
  • LLM Reasoning
  • High Performance Compiting
  • Deep Learning
  • Medical Image Analysis
  • Transfer Learning
